this app directly links the person in need of help to a rehab centre and this whole process is done confidentially which makes the concerned person feel safe without the pressure of having to worry about the inevitable, "what will people think?? " question.

mental and physical well being is now only a single click away with the help of this app, without requiring many formalities. The concerned person can simply put in basic information about themselves like name, phone no., e-mail id, etc and choose a package of their choice pertaining to their budget; providing a simple solution.

before choosing the package, the person can also opt for counselling by requesting for the same in the chat option. After talking about their problems they will be required to fill a questionnaire made specially for them which will help us better understand and analyse their problems to provide helpful solutions.

all necessary tests like blood tests, kidney function tests, liver function tests, etc will be conducted by us. All details related to the tests will be communicated to the person way before the actual test. The person will be assigned a centre which will be inside a hospital and get the specific test done, which will be completely safe and professional. They will be able to get their results either online or they can collect the report from the centre itself, whatever they find fit.

we will hire graduates who have majored in clinical psychology to counsel those who have opted for the free counselling option and ate dealing with mental issues which will not only help people get the help and guidance they need but also help young people get employed, gain experience and earn a living.
